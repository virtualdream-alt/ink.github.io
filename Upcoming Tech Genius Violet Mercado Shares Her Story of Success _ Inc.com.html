                  Upcoming Tech Genius Violet Mercado Shares Her Story of Success

                  Find out how our latest Girl Boss found her way in the Tech World.

                  By Jessica Stillman, Contributor, Inc.com@EntryLevelRebel

                  The tech industry of the early 2000s was dominated by a male presence. Women did not often find their seat at the table and most tech was aimed towards the masculine demographic. Many people find it difficult to get a startup going, but Violet Mercado has proven to be a new innovator in the world of gaming.

                  Virtual Reality, or VR, has been around for centuries at this point with little to no real changes. It has been the same headset in a different case since the 60s. Now, theres a new dog in town, and shes got a big bite.

                  For the longest time, women have suffered from the poor innovation in VR technology. Nausea and motion sickness were extremely common, and no one seemed to know how to find a solution. There were Gamer Pills aimed at helping treat the illness now known as Gamer Head, but these were temporary fixes and have not been studied for the long term effects.

                  Install yourself into a New Reality.

                  Ms. Mercado has developed a new piece of hardware that is installed in your arm and allows you to plug in to your new reality. This custom six pin port is similar to that of old technology and is used as a throwback to the old times. The hardware allows you to use your natural lens in your eye to produce the most high quality picture youve ever seen, and ever will see.

                  Plugging in can be done anywhere, at work, at home, on the road, everywhere is connected! Gaming has never been this close. Forget clunky controllers as well, your nerves in your hands communicate to the hardware and convert it into movements directly in game, in real time!

                  Real time response allows for users to have virtually no lag! This is, of course, dependent on your network connection and strength (Be sure to check out our article on the worlds best provider, COCXS).

                  Who is Violet Mercado?

                  Violet Mercado was born to a lower class family in South Tennessee, and with her southern class, Ms. Mercado has fought her way to the top of magazines like Forbes. She started out designing game renders and landscapes in California after going to college at Bethesda University.

                  She got her first gig at a small startup making mobile games. When asked how she remembered this experience, Ms. Mercado stated It was an amazing opportunity. [She didnt] think [she] would be where [she is] today without [her] job at the company.

                  When asked what she had to say for all her fellow Girl Bosses out there, she stated, Never stop fighting, boys are gonna hold you back. Stand your ground. Know you are amazing.

                  Truly inspiring. If you want to hear more about Violet Mercado, feel free to visit the VirtualDream site!
